Usoamic - smart contract on Ethereum blockchain

Swap UCoin/IslaCoin to USO

Your IslaCoin and / or UCoin can be exchanged for Usoamic through the swap platform (exchanger.islacoin.net and swap.usoamic.io).

If your coins are on official web-wallets (islawallet.net and wallet.ucoin.name), then you can immediately withdraw Usoamic from them (the swap was done automatically).

Description

This smart contract allows you to:

  • Transfer tokens.
  • Offer your ideas for the development of the project.
  • Add your notes, which will be stored on the blockchain.
  • Exchange USO to ETH (if funds are available at the contract address).
  • Make purchases in applications that support this feature.
