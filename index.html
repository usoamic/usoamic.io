Usoamic - smart contract on Ethereum blockchain

Swap UCoin/IslaCoin to USO

Your IslaCoin and/or UCoin can be exchanged for Usoamic through the swap platform (IslaCoin Exchanger and/or Swap Platform for UCoin).

If your coins are on official web-wallets (IslaWallet and UCoin Wallet), then you can immediately withdraw Usoamic from them (the swap has been made automatically).

Description

This smart-contract allows to:

  • Transfer tokens.
  • Offer your ideas for the development of the project.
  • Add your notes, which will be stored on the blockchain.
  • Exchange USO to ETH (if there are available funds at the contract address).
  • Make purchases in applications that support this feature.
